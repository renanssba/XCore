
SHORT DESCRIPTION:
Be the Cupid and bring together any couple you dream of! Help humans develop their relationships by living magical moments through dates and unlock couple skills in this RPG / Dating Sim game.



FULL DESCRIPTION:


CHOOSE WHICH PEOPLE GET TOGETHER
Experience being the Cupid and bringing together any couple you want! Choose which people make the cutest or more interesting couple during each chapter.


INVESTIGATE YOUR HUMANS TO MATCH THEM
Discover the human's tastes and personalities by investigating their houses and routines. Then help develop their relationships by using gifts and setting up the perfect dates for them.


COMPLETE RPG GAMEPLAY
Take your human couple through turn-based battles, make the perfect skill build and go on procedural date locations. Each couple has their own unique skilltree, that represent their relationship and personalities.


MAKE YOUR DREAM COUPLE A REALITY
Create your character's appearances, personalities and skills. Make the couple of you dreans to life, even with characters from other anime and games or your own original characters!


MAKE THE GAME YOUR OWN
Experience entirely new situations and characters created by other players or create your own and share to the community. The game has high support for mods and player generated content.


A CUTE CUPID STORY
Help the protagonist Fertiliel meet many new friends and pay her bills in the strict, orderly world of angels through the Story Mode.





Cupid Simulator features:

-multiple routes for every chapter
-dating sim mechanics
-give gifts to girls
-investigate girls rooms
-turn-based RPG battles
-customizeable characters
-different skilltree for each couple of humans
-different paths to ship for each mission
-high support for mods and player-created content
